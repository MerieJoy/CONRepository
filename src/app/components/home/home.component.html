About Us

Welcome to our church! We are a community dedicated to faith, worship, and service. Below, you can learn about our core values, mission, and vision.

Core Values

  • We Are A Christian People

    The Holy Spirit plays a transformative role in the lives of believers, shaping them into individuals characterized by profound love for one another, steadfast spiritual discipline, unwavering commitment to ethical and moral purity, genuine compassion for those in need, and a relentless pursuit of justice. This transformative power fosters a community grounded in these core principles, guiding every action and decision.

  • We Are A Holiness People

    The Church is dedicated to fostering a vibrant and nurturing disciple-making community, deeply rooted in the Wesleyan-Holiness tradition. This tradition emphasizes a transformative journey of spiritual growth and sanctification, where members are encouraged to live out their faith actively through personal holiness, community service, and continuous learning. The Church strives to create an environment where individuals can grow spiritually, build strong relationships, and engage in meaningful outreach, all while upholding the values and practices of the Wesleyan-Holiness heritage.

  • We Are A Missional People

    The Church is deeply committed to engaging in compassionate outreach that addresses the needs of our community and beyond. This involves actively serving those who are marginalized, vulnerable, or in distress, while promoting social justice and support. In addition, the Church emphasizes comprehensive biblical learning, encouraging members to deepen their understanding of Scripture through robust teaching, study groups, and personal reflection. This dual focus on outreach and education aims to empower individuals to live out their faith meaningfully and effectively, fostering both personal growth and community transformation.

Our Mission

“To be a Christ-like disciple and to make Christ-like disciples in Tanza and beyond.”

Our mission is to embody the teachings of Christ by striving to be true, Christ-like disciples in our community of Tanza and extending this commitment beyond. This mission is the cornerstone of our identity and purpose, defining who we are, why we exist, and the core reason for our being. We are dedicated to living out the example of Jesus Christ through acts of love, humility, and service, and we are equally committed to guiding others in their journey to becoming Christ-like disciples. Our goal is to foster a nurturing environment where individuals are equipped with the spiritual tools and support they need to grow in their faith, share the message of Christ, and positively impact the world around them. This mission drives every aspect of our church life, shaping our programs, outreach efforts, and community engagements, and inspiring us to fulfill our calling to spread the love and teachings of Christ to all we encounter.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a impactful community by focusing on key areas that drive our mission forward. We aim to:

  • Engage our community.

    Actively involve and connect with local individuals and groups to build relationships and foster a supportive community.

  • Establish strong spiritual foundations

    Establish strong spiritual foundationsCreate a solid basis for spiritual growth through teaching, worship, and personal development.

  • Equip believers to serve.

    Provide resources and training to help individuals effectively use their gifts and talents in service to others.

  • Empower disciples to go and make disciples.

    Encourage and support believers in sharing their faith and mentoring others in their spiritual journey.
